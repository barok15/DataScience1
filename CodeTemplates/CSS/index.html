USING SPOTIFY TO MEASURE THE POPULARITY OF OLDER MUSIC
BY MATT DANIELS, EDITOR

This is a story about proving, with data, that No Diggity by Blackstreet is timeless

Until recently, it was impossible to measure the popularity of older music. Billboard charts and album sales only tell us about a song's popularity at the time of its release.'

But now we have Spotify, a buffet of all music, new and old. Tracks with fewer plays are fading into obscurity. And those with more plays are remaining in the cultural ether.

20 years have passed since N Diggity's release. Its popularity on Spotify, relative to every other song form the 90s, is a strong signal for whether it will be remembered by our children's children. So let's examine every song that ever charted, 1990-1999, and rank them by number of plays on Spotify, today.
